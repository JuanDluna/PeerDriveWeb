PeerDrive Help & FAQ

Get answers to common questions about using PeerDrive.

PeerDrive is a distributed student transportation platform that helps students share rides to and from campus or nearby locations. The platform connects drivers and passengers based on matching schedules and optimized routes.

Students can register as either drivers or passengers. Drivers offer rides based on their schedules, while passengers search for available rides. The system optimizes routes and sends real-time notifications to both drivers and passengers.

To register as a driver, provide your route details (departure point, destination, and schedule). To book a ride as a passenger, simply enter your start and end points to find drivers offering rides along matching routes.

Yes! PeerDrive is available for Android devices and web browsers, providing flexibility for both drivers and passengers to access the platform from any device.

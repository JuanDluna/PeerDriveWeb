About PeerDrive

Discover how PeerDrive is revolutionizing student transportation.

Our Mission

PeerDrive aims to foster a collaborative environment for students to share rides within or outside the campus, addressing mobility issues and promoting sustainable transportation.

How It Works

Our platform connects student drivers with passengers needing transportation through optimized routes and real-time notifications. Drivers can offer rides, and passengers can book trips based on matching schedules and routes.

Key Features

  • Distributed Storage: User data is securely stored using a dual-server approach with NFS and Google Drive.
  • Route Optimization: Google Maps API ensures efficient route planning.
  • Multi-Platform Access: Available on Android devices and web browsers for flexibility.

Our Impact

PeerDrive maximizes vehicle usage, reduces transportation costs, and minimizes environmental impact by encouraging carpooling among students. It fosters a sense of community and enhances mobility for everyone.
